Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 3, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Timestamp Tracker collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Location Data

When you start a shift, we collect your GPS location data including:

  • Latitude and longitude coordinates
  • Timestamp of each location point
  • Location accuracy information
  • Reverse-geocoded address (street name, city)

Why: To track your movement during shifts and generate location trails for verification and reporting purposes.

1.2 Photos and Images

When you capture photos during a shift, we collect:

  • The photo image itself
  • Timestamp when photo was taken
  • Location where photo was captured (if available)
  • Watermarked metadata (date, time, location, staff name, site name)

Why: To provide timestamped evidence of your presence at specific locations during your shift.

1.3 Shift Information

We collect information about your shifts including:

  • Staff name (as entered by you)
  • Site name/location
  • Shift start and end times
  • Shift duration
  • Notes added during shifts
  • Pair codes for live viewing

1.4 Device Information

We may collect basic device information:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App version
  • IP address (for server requests)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide shift tracking services - Record and display your location trail during shifts
  • Generate reports - Create PDF reports with photos, locations, and shift details
  • Enable live viewing - Allow authorized viewers to see your live location during active shifts
  • Improve our service - Analyze usage patterns to enhance app performance and features
  • Communicate with you - Send important updates about the service (rare)

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

3.1 Who Can See Your Data

  • You: Full access to all your shift data, photos, and reports
  • Authorized viewers: People you share your live link or pair code with can view your location and photos during active shifts
  • Your employer/client: If you share shift reports, they can access the information in those reports
  • Service providers: We use Railway (hosting), MySQL (database) to securely store and process your data

3.2 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

3.3 Legal Compliance

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data Is Stored

  • Location: United States (Railway cloud infrastructure)
  • Database: MySQL with automated backups
  • Photos: Stored as base64-encoded data in database or cloud storage

4.2 Security Measures

We protect your data using:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure database with authentication
  • Unique pair codes that expire when shifts end
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
Note: No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

Free Tier: Shift data retained for 7 days, then automatically deleted

Pro Tier: Shift data retained for 90 days

Business Tier: Shift data retained for 1 year

You can delete your data at any time from the app settings.

6. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you are in the European Union or EEA, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we have about you
  • Correction: Request corrections to inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request permanent deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format (JSON/CSV)
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Object: Object to data processing in certain situations
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time (though this may limit app functionality)

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Children's Privacy

Timestamp Tracker is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do:

  • We'll update the "Last Updated" date at the top
  • For significant changes, we'll notify you via the app
  • Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Railway: Cloud hosting - Privacy Policy
  • OpenStreetMap Nominatim: Reverse geocoding - Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps API: Map display (viewer only) - Privacy Policy
