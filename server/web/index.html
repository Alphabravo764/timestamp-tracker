How it works.

From arrival to verified reporting in four simple steps.

1

Check In

Start your site visit with a single tap. We record your GPS location and device time to mark the beginning of your shift.

2

Document Your Work

Add notes and capture timestamped photos as you work. All evidence is recorded with GPS coordinates and timestamps for verification.

3

Share Live or Export

Share a live tracking link with clients, or generate a professional PDF report with all locations, photos, and timestamps.

4

Verify Anywhere

Every report includes a unique verification link. Clients can validate the evidence themselves on our official servers.

STAMPIA is designed for operational verification and reporting. It does not replace contractual, legal, or regulatory approval processes.
